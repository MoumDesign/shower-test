Welcome to MeScapes Shower Practice.

One of the most efficient ways to lower your personal energy consumption, and carbon footprint, is to take shorter showers.

On average, we spend 800 kWh every year on showers. That's enough energy to power your phone for over 100 years.

For every minute we shorten our showers by, we can expect to save 120 kWh a year, which is equivalent of about 300 SEK.

Evening Wind Down Shower Practice.

This practice takes about 5 minutes to complete and does not include washing your hair. It consists of five phases:

Intro
30s
Soak
60s
Soap
30s
Wash
120s
Dry
60s

Before we begin, please do the following:

  1. Turn off silent mode and set your volume to the highest level.
  2. Turn off auto-lock in settings and set the screen brightness to the maximum level.
  3. Make your bathroom as dark and cozy as possible.
